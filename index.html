Welcome to Local Creative Minds!

We here at LCM (Local Creative Minds) are passionate about finding and exposing local talent!

Are you a singer or writer? Would you like for your voice to be heard across South Africa, even the entire world? Look no further, we are dedicated to voicing your thoughts. We believe in YOU.It is about that do to. Allow this to the be the stage that you have never had the courage to step on. Feel free to reach out to other Local Minds in the search for you muse.


We are a community of artists, growing and learning from one another. This is not just another page for posting, no, this is your safe space, your time to shine and let hear your inner artist.


As Albert Einstein once said, "Creativity is intelligence having fun". So let us all embrace the opportunity to have fun.


Go ahead put up the first of many posts and let the world hear your voice.
